Travelling into Nigeria

Travellers coming into Nigeria are required to visit the Nigeria International Travel Portal and complete the screening form. You'll need to select AlphaCare as your preferred test centre. You will receive an email with a link to schedule a test appointment. Click the link and provide your preferred test date and time. At the test center, your sample will be collected and analyzed and you'll receive your test results in approximately four hours.

Traveling Out of Nigeria

If you're travelling out of Nigeria or need a COVID test for any other purpose, you may schedule your test directly with us.

Symptomatic Testing

If you have been exposed to an infected person, or suspect you have COVID infection, get tested now and find out your COVID status. You can also do a follow up test to confirm you have built up immunity following an infection.
