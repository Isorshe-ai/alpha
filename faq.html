BEFORE TESTING

  • Visit The appointment section of our website to book an appointment and make payment for your test. Note that your appointment is not booked untill you have made payment.
  • On the test day, visit the sample collection site with your BOOKING REFERENCE or for a home care service await the arrival of our laboratory scientist for sample collection
  • Ensure you wear a face mask at all times when at our testing facility.
  • For COVID-19 tests for the purpose of travel, kindly ensure that your booking is within the testing window of the airline you are travelling with and no later than 48 hours before your proposed travel date to ensure the availability of your results prior to departure.
  • While coming for your appointment, come with your booking reference and a valid identification card.
    • Accepted identification cards include:
      • -National ID Card
      -Passport
      -Voters Card
      -Driver’s Licence

DURING TESTING

  • Kindly follow all instructions given at the site on where to drive through & park.
  • Do not come out of your car.
  • If you do not come with a car, you will be directed to the designated waiting area. Please stay seated until it is your turn.
  • Note that testing will be on a first come first served basis for patients who are booked for the same time slot.

AFTER TESTING

  • Your test results will be sent to you via email
  • As this is a notifiable disease which has the risk of contagion and a disease of public health concern, we are legally required to notify the health authorities of our results.
  • Stamped results can be obtained at the clinic for those that require one for the purpose of travel. Inform the receptionist at the gate that you would like to have your test stamped.
  • Note that we do not print out results at the clinic. You will need to bring your printed COVID-19 test result with you.
